# Geo-Aware MRO Decision Intelligence System

AI-powered geo-aware MRO decision intelligence system combining demand forecasting, geopolitical supply risk modeling, and multi-echelon inventory optimization.


**Demand Forecasting + Geopolitical Supply Risk + Multi-Echelon Inventory Optimization + Cost Savings Modeling**

### Business Problem
With 19+ years in large-scale Maintenance, Repair & Overhaul (MRO) operations, managing spares demand, inventory, and supply risk from multiple international origins as well as Domestic origin is critical for maintaining high asset availability while controlling costs.

This project builds a geo-aware decision intelligence system that integrates demand forecasting, SKU criticality classification, geopolitical risk modeling, multi-echelon network, and cost savings hypothesis to support resilient MRO supply chain decisions.

### Architecture Layers
1. Demand Forecasting
2. SKU Classification (Vital/Essential/Desirable, Cost, Consumption)
3. Geopolitical Supply Risk
4. Multi-Echelon Network (Central → Regional → Forward)
5. **Cost Savings & Hypothesis**
6. Inventory Optimization (MILP)
7. Simulation & What-if Analysis
8. Decision Intelligence (RAG)

### Tech Stack
- Python, pandas, numpy, scikit-learn
- Prophet, XGBoost
- PuLP (MILP)
- MLflow
- Streamlit
- Docker, FastAPI (planned)

### Expected Impact
- Improved demand accuracy and service levels
- Risk-adjusted inventory decisions
- Quantified cost savings through scenario modeling
